About Me

My name is Rahdee Salie. I am a Graphic Designer, Photographer and an UX/UI developer. I was born and raised in the beautiful city of Cape Town.


I strive to create professional, vibrant and elegant works of art. I am always up for a challenge, which in the end motivates me to always do my best.


I am hard working, motivated by continuous learning and a teamplayer. I am passionate about producing attractive and detailed work and I am confident that I will bring fresh, creative and innovative ideas to the table. I am a quick learner who is good at multitasking.I always look for opportunities to broaden my horizons in every way possible.


I began my coding journey in 2022 at UWC, where I applied to the Samsung Future Innovation Lab Programme and attained a Certificate of Competency in Introduction to multi-platform software development and a certificate for Digital Social Innovation for Impact.
